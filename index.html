      Student View - How to View Your Data

      1. Find Scholarships: Browse all available scholarships with filters by name, amount, and GPA requirements
      2. View Details: Click "View Details" on any scholarship card to see full information and deadline
      3. Apply for Scholarship: Click "Apply Now" to submit your application with your information
      4. My Applications: View all your submitted applications and their current status
      5. Export Data: Download your applications as PDF for your records

      Data Storage

      Note: All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

      • Data persists when you close and reopen the browser
      • Each browser/device has separate data
      • Clearing browser cache will delete all stored data
      • To backup your data, use the Export to PDF feature
